# 📊 E=commerce KPI & Operations Dashboard — Excel Project

An interactive business intelligence dashboard built entirely in Microsoft Excel, designed to analyze and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) across orders, sales, customer behavior, and product performance. This is a guided project completed as part of my data analytics learning journey.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a fully interactive KPI dashboard that gives a 360-degree view of business performance. The dashboard is built to answer real business questions — from tracking weekly sales trends to understanding customer satisfaction and shipping efficiency — all within Excel using pivot tables, charts, and slicers.

## 🔍 Dashboard Sections

**1. Last 13 Week Trends — Quantity & Amount**
- Dual-axis line chart tracking weekly quantity and revenue side by side over a 13-week period
- Helps identify sales patterns, peaks, and drops over time

**2. Which Products are Popular? Breakdown by Gender**
- Horizontal bar chart showing quantity sold per product (T-Shirts, Sneakers, Bikinis, Sundresses, Hoodies, and more)
- Donut chart showing gender split: 52% Female, 39% Male, 3% Other
- Filters by product and customer gender

**3. Order Source by Gender Heatmap**
- Matrix table showing order distribution (%) across platforms: App, Instagram, Partner App, Target.com, and Website
- Broken down by Female, Male, Other, and Unknown gender values
- Highlights which channels perform best for which demographics

**4. How Long We Take to Ship?**
- Bar chart showing transaction count by days to deliver
- Majority of orders delivered within 1–2 days, reflecting strong logistics performance

**5. How Satisfied Are Our Customers?**
- Grouped bar chart showing customer rating distribution (1 to 5 stars) across January, February, and March
- Helps track satisfaction trends month over month

**6. How Many They Buy?**
- Bar chart breaking down order frequency by quantity range (1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6–10, More than 10)
- Most customers purchase 2 items per order (540 transactions)

**7. Product Quantity in California State**
- Horizontal bar chart showing product-level quantity performance specifically in California
- Useful for regional inventory and demand planning

## 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)

- **Order Mode** — Filter by Instagram, Partner App, Target.com, Website
- **Gender Value** — Filter by Female, Male, Other, Unknown

All charts and KPI cards update dynamically based on slicer selections.
